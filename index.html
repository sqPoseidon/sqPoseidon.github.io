About

I'm a Post-Doctoral Associate at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Cornell University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhiru Zhang since Fall 2022. Previously, I received my Ph.D. degree from the Dept. of CSE, CUHK, under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu in Jul. 2022. My research interests include machine learning in EDA, design space exploration, and Deep Neural Network hardware acceleration. My work has been recognized with an ICCAD Best Paper Award, Bronze Medal of ICCAD Student Research Competition, and a Best Paper Award Nomination of DATE.

I will join the School of Micro-Nano Electronics, Zhejiang University, as a tenure-track assistant professor starting from Sept. 2023. I have openings for highly motivated students and Research Assistants with background in computer science and engineering, electronic engineering, applied mathematics, or other related fields. Please contact me with your CV and transcript if you’re interested.
